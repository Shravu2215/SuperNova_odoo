# 🚀 AssetFlow

### Smart Enterprise Asset & Resource Management System

**Built by Team SuperNova ⚡ | Odoo Hackathon 2026**

---

## 📌 About

AssetFlow is a modern Enterprise Asset & Resource Management System designed to simplify the way organizations track, allocate, maintain, and audit their assets.

From preventing double allocation to managing maintenance workflows and resource bookings, AssetFlow provides a secure, real-time platform that helps organizations stay organized and efficient.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔐 Secure JWT Authentication
- 👥 Role-Based Access Control
- 📦 Asset Lifecycle Management
- 📅 Smart Resource Booking
- 🚫 Double Allocation Prevention
- 🔧 Maintenance Request Workflow
- 📊 Real-Time Dashboard
- 🔔 Instant Notifications
- 📋 Audit & Asset Tracking
- 📈 Analytics & Reports

## 🎯 Why AssetFlow?

Managing company assets shouldn't be complicated.

AssetFlow automates tracking, allocation, maintenance, bookings, and audits while ensuring data integrity with database-level validation and role-based security.
